# Target identification memo: p53 in colorectal cancer

TP53 is a tumour suppressor that is frequently inactivated in colorectal cancer [1].
The p53 protein participates in the cellular response to DNA damage [1].
The recommended starting dose of the candidate compound was 5 mg once daily [1].
A pivotal trial reported complete remission in every treated patient [2].
